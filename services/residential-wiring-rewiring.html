Common questions

How do I know if my home needs rewiring?

Signs include frequent breaker trips, flickering lights, burning smells, or outlets that are warm to the touch. If your home is over 40 years old, it's worth an inspection.

How long does a full rewiring take?

A typical whole-home rewiring can take 3-7 days depending on the size and complexity of the home.
