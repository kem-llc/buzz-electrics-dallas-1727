Common questions

How do I know if my home needs rewiring?

Signs include flickering lights, frequent breaker trips, and outlets that feel warm. If your home is over 40 years old, it's wise to have an inspection.

How long does a full rewiring take?

It depends on the size of your home. Most projects take 3-5 days. We minimize disruption by working efficiently and cleaning up daily.
