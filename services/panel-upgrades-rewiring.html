Panel Upgrades & Rewiring

Upgrade your electrical panel or rewire older homes for safety and capacity.

Schedule this service

Overview

Older homes often have outdated wiring and undersized panels that can't handle modern loads. We specialize in upgrading to 200-amp panels, replacing knob-and-tube wiring, and bringing your entire system up to current code. Our team handles all permits and inspections for a hassle-free experience.

What's included

  • Increased capacity for appliances and electronics
  • Reduced risk of electrical fires
  • Improved home resale value
  • Permit and inspection management included

How it works

1

Assessment

We evaluate your current panel and wiring, then design a custom upgrade plan.

2

Installation

We safely disconnect old components and install new panel and wiring per code.

3

Final Inspection

We schedule city inspection and ensure everything passes with flying colors.
