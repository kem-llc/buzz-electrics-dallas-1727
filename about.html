Our story

Your Trusted Dallas Electricians

To provide safe, reliable, and affordable electrical services that exceed expectations — every time.

Buzz Electrics was founded in 2008 by a team of master electricians who saw a need for honest, high-quality electrical work in Dallas. Starting with a single truck and a commitment to excellence, we built a reputation for solving tough electrical problems with precision and care. Today, we serve hundreds of homeowners and businesses across the metroplex, from historic homes in Lakewood to modern offices in Uptown.

Our team undergoes continuous training to stay current with the latest codes and technologies. We believe in doing the job right the first time, with transparent communication and fair pricing. Whether it's a simple outlet repair or a complex commercial installation, we treat every project with the same dedication.
