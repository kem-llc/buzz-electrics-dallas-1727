Our story

Your Trusted Dallas Electricians Since 2010

To provide safe, reliable, and affordable electrical services with a focus on customer satisfaction and craftsmanship.

Buzz Electrics was founded in 2010 by master electrician Mike Thompson, who saw a need for honest, high-quality electrical work in Dallas. Starting with just a truck and a handful of tools, Mike built a reputation for showing up on time, explaining every step, and never cutting corners. Today, Buzz Electrics serves hundreds of homes and businesses across the Dallas metroplex, with a team of licensed, background-checked electricians who share Mike's commitment to excellence.

We've grown from a one-man operation to a full-service electrical company, but our core values remain the same: transparency, safety, and old-fashioned customer service. Every job, big or small, gets the same meticulous attention to detail. We're proud of our 5.0 rating and the trust our community places in us. When you call Buzz Electrics, you're getting a partner who cares about your home or business as much as you do.
