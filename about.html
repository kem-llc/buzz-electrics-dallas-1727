Our story

Bringing Power and Safety to Dallas Homes & Businesses

Our mission is to provide exceptional electrical services that prioritize safety, reliability, and customer satisfaction. We believe in honest communication, fair pricing, and craftsmanship that lasts.

Buzz Electrics was founded by a team of master electricians who saw a need for trustworthy service in Dallas. Tired of fly-by-night operators and shoddy work, they set out to build a company that puts quality first. Starting with just a truck and a commitment to excellence, they grew through word-of-mouth and repeat business.

Today, Buzz Electrics serves hundreds of residential and commercial clients across the Dallas metro area. Our team continues to invest in training, tools, and technology to stay ahead of industry standards. We're proud to be a local business that treats every job like it's our own home.
